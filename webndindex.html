WEBND SOCIETY

AboutUs

The Web and Design Society of IIT Bhubaneswar has the credit of managing and designing all the websites of the institute. The Web and Design Society (WebnD) at IIT Bhubaneswar (IIT BBS) is a student-run technical club focused on web development,UI/UX design, and creative tech, known for organizing events like "Frontend Battle," workshops (DesignX) with industry experts, and fostering a vibrant tech community for students interested in building digital experiences. They actively promote skills in Figma, typography, color theory, and design systems, engaging students with competitions and industry insights.

Key Activities & Focus Areas:

Workshops: Conduct design sessions (DesignX) on UI/UX, Figma, visual design, and typography. Competitions: Organize events like the "Frontend Battle," attracting thousands of participants.

Skill Development: Focus on core web/design skills: UI/UX, frontend, typography, Figma, design systems.

Community Building: Create platforms for students to connect and learn.

The society where passionate developers connect, ideate and build together!
